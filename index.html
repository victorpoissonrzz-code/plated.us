Our Story

Born from a
dinner table
in New York.

One man's obsession with starred kitchens, and the idea that a great meal shouldn't end when you leave the restaurant.

The Founder

Victor,
hospitality at heart

Victor grew up between France and the United States, spending his formative years at tables that mattered, restaurants where every plate told a story, where the chef's name was spoken with the same reverence as a painter or a couturier.

Settling in New York City, he became a regular at the city's finest starred restaurants. Each dinner was an experience he wanted to hold onto, the weight of a particular knife, the geometry of a handmade ceramic, the fragrance of a sauce he could never quite replicate at home.

He realised he was not alone. Every great restaurant has guests who ask "where can I buy this?", and too often, the answer is nowhere. The souvenir of a remarkable meal existed only in memory.

Victor founded Plated, a platform that gives top restaurants a shoppable boutique, and gives their guests a way to bring a piece of that experience home. From their kitchen to yours.

Victor
Founder & CEO, Plated, New York

What we
believe in

Three principles that shape every decision at Plated.

01
The meal doesn't end at dessert

A great restaurant experience lives in memory, and in the objects it leaves behind. We believe the best souvenirs are the ones that bring the kitchen into your home.

02
Craft deserves a stage

Chefs and their suppliers create extraordinary things, ceramics, linens, sauces, tools, that rarely reach beyond the restaurant walls. Plated opens that door.

03
Prestige without compromise

We built Plated to match the standards of the restaurants it serves. Every detail, editorial, typographic, commercial, is held to the same exacting level.

A restaurant on your
table at home

Join the chefs already on Plated, and let your guests take a piece of your kitchen with them.
